Betty Chang


I build and invest in solutions that move the needle on human sustainability, with specific interests in healthcare, climate, cities and gender issues. I’m currently the Chief of Staff at Capable Health and a Venture Fellow at Susa Ventures.

My happy place is using — and learning — new technologies (input) to build loveable products (output) that do good (outcome): I’ve crafted strategies to increase STEM education at Luminary Labs, stood up an investment fund for the gender team at UNICEF, shipped features to improve internet security at Intel, and researched how to make our cities more vibrant places to live at Sidewalk Labs.

I have a penchant for contemporary art, DIY crafts, vintage lamps, and dim sum.
